Hilsha

An app that helps you to find cateres who sells ethnic Bangladeshi comfort food in Toronto

Background

Few years back, I migrated from Bangladesh to Canada to persue my canadian dream. After coming here, the thing I missed most from my homeland is the food. Often, I have hopped around Toronto looking for ethnic Bangladeshi food to serve my cravings. My personal experience and background has helped me to empathize with other immigrants who miss their ethnic comfort food. Very recently, when I was doing "Google UX Design Certification", I took the opportunity to hone my skills to design a product that serve the need of Bangladeshi immigrants in Toronto.

Role

User Research, Synthesis, Ideation, Testing, Interactive Prototype
